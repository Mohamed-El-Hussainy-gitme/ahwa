# Phase A-3 — Reports Readability Refactor

## Goal
Make reports easier to read without expanding the operational domain.

## Implemented
- Restructure the reports screen into a lighter hierarchy:
  - summary hero
  - quick insight strip
  - detail tabs: summary / products / staff / notes
- Sort products by net sales and staff by payment total for faster reading.
- Reduce vertical overload by hiding full sections behind detail tabs.
- Keep complaints and item issues visible in a dedicated notes view.
- Keep deferred in a separate simplified view.

## Result
The reports page is now closer to an owner-facing summary page rather than a long stacked raw dump.
